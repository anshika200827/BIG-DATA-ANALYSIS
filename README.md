##DESCRIPTION#


Customers Sales Analysis — Power BI Dashboard

This project performs a sales data analysis on a customers dataset containing country-wise and quarter-wise sales figures spanning the years 2011 to 2014, using Microsoft Excel for data organisation and Power BI Desktop for building an interactive visual dashboard. The dataset is structured across two tables — Country-wise Sales and Quarter Sales — covering regions including Technology, Furniture, and Office Supplies categories across global markets such as Central, South, North, Oceania, Southeast Asia, North Asia, EMEA, Africa, Central Asia, West, East, Caribbean, and Canada. The line chart visual reveals that the Grand Total and Technology category lead sales by a significant margin reaching up to 13 million, followed by Furniture and Office Supplies with a steep drop-off across remaining regions, clearly highlighting the top-performing markets. The pie chart breaks down sales by quarter and year showing that Grand Total accounts for 33.33% of the share, with individual quarters — Qtr1, Qtr2, Qtr3, and Qtr4 — and years 2011 through 2014 each contributing between approximately 5% to 11% of total sales, providing a clear picture of how revenue is distributed across time periods. Together the two visuals provide a comprehensive overview of sales performance by geography and seasonality, making it easy to identify peak quarters and high-revenue regions at a glance.
